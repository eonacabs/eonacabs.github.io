About Me

My name is Eona Daphnie E. Cabacungan. I am second-year multimedia art student from FEU Institute of Technology. I live in Pasig City, surrounded by the perfect ambiance of the city. My hobbies are playing games, playing the guitar and crocheting. I love the color green and color red. I love art and I enjoy expressing myself through various artistic mediums. I love drawing and painting digitally and a little traditionally. I also love designing posters. I aspire to be a professional graphic designer and share my works in the future.
